FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions.

Q1: **What is the Staff System?**

A: The Staff System is a user-friendly platform designed to facilitate the registration process for new staff members. It allows users to add, delete, update and view manage their profiles.

Q2: **What information do I need to provide during the registration process?**

A: You will need to provide your personal details, such as your name, email, and phone number.

Q3: **How can I update my personal information after registering?**

Navigate to the View Staff page and you can click the Update button.

Q4: **Is my personal information secure in the Staff Registration System?**

A: Yes, your personal information is secure in the Staff System. We follow best practices in data protection and privacy, ensuring that your information remains safe and confidential at all times.
